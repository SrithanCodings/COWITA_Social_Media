About COWITA SOCIAL MEDIA

COWITA SOCIAL MEDIA is an online platform designed to increase the social contact between its users. Full formed as Community Organized Web Interfaced Texting Application, it mainly focuses on enhancing the user experience by providing multiple features like:

  • It provides double-security feature of login with username and password along with face recognized login system
  • It has an option to communicate with other users by sending messages
  • You can also like the posts sent by other users increasing contact with them
  • You get personalized results according to your following friends
  • You can bookmark and also comment on posts
  • You can video call or voice call with any friend or a group of friends by using InstaChat feature
  • You get instant notifications when your friend follows you or posts a post
  • You can also change your settings anytime, anywhere
  • You can search for anyone's profile by using the search menu
